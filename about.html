About ROI Technologies

Who We Are

ROI Technologies is a consulting firm providing innovative and diverse information solutions. We have built our reputation on a deep understanding of the life sciences industry — combining business process expertise with practical, results-oriented IT delivery.

With over 15 years of continuous service to leading pharmaceutical organizations including Pfizer, we have developed a unique perspective on the challenges and opportunities that define the industry. We bring that experience to every engagement, large or small.

Our work spans IT Consulting Services and Training, Field Force Realignments, Sales-related Processes, System and Data Conversions, System Integration, and Process Transformations — always applied with a clear eye on how technology serves the business.
